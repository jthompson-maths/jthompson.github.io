Hello

I am currently studying for a PhD in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Western Australia. My research interests are in elliptic/parabolic partial differential equations (PDEs), particulary in integro-differential equations. Currently, I am working on projects in nonlocal overdetermined problems and regularity theory for nonlocal elliptic PDE. My supervisors are Enrico Valdinoci (principle supervisor), Serena Dipierro, and Lyle Noakes.

Office: G14, Mathematics building.
